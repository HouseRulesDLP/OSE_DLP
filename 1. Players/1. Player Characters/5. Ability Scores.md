# Ability Scores
A character’s score in each ability determines whether they have any bonuses or penalties associated with various actions in the game. The tables below list the modifiers associated with each ability score, with the effects described below.[^1]

## Strength (STR)
Brawn, muscle power, and physical might.

- **Melee/Hurled:** This is applied to attack and damage rolls with melee and thrown weapons.
- **Skill:** This adjusts the target number when brute force applies to a skill check.
- **Test of Strength:** The target number to force open a stuck door, move a rusted lever or similar feat requiring muscle power (see ***[[5. Dungeon Adventuring|Dungeon Adventuring]]***).

## Intelligence (INT)
Learning, memory, and reasoning.

- **Languages:** Denotes the number of languages the character can speak, how well they speak and whether or not they are literate.
- **Skill:** This adjusts the target number when reasoning or knowledge applies to a skill check.
- **Test of Intellect:** The target number to solve a riddle, decipher a simple code or similar task requiring reason and learning (see ***[[5. Dungeon Adventuring|Dungeon Adventuring]]***).
- **Invocation Dice:** Indicates the number of additional [[Invocation Dice]] arcane casters receive (See ***[[9. Magic|Magic]]***).
- **Chance to Learn Arcane Spell:** The chance on a d12 for an arcane caster to learn a new spell.

## Wisdom (WIS)
Insight, common sense, perception and willpower.

- **Willpower Adjustment:** Is applied to saving throws (see ***Saving Throws***) versus mind control (including [[1 - Charm Person|charm person]], [[1 - Remove Fear|fear]], [[3 - Hold Person|hold person]], possession, suggestion and so forth).
- **Skill:** This adjusts the target number when willpower or perception applies to a skill check.
- **Test of Willpower:** The target number to keep your cool in a crisis, spot something hidden, track an animal or similar task requiring willpower or perception (see ***[[5. Dungeon Adventuring|Dungeon Adventuring]]***).
- **Bonus Prayers:** This is the number of extra [[Prayers]] divine casters receive (See ***[[9. Magic|Magic]]***).
- **Granted Miracle Bonus:** The bonus to the [[Miracle Granted]] check divine casters receive on level-up.

## Dexterity (DEX)
Agility, reflexes, speed, and balance.

- **AC:** Modifies the character’s AC.
- **Missile/Finesse:** Applied to attack rolls and damage rolls with ranged and finesse weapons.
- **Initiative:** Modifies the character’s initiative roll (see ***[[10. Combat|Combat]]***).
- **Skill:** This adjusts the target number when agility, balance or reflexes would apply to a skill check.
- **Test of Dexterity:** The chance on a d12 to jump a 10-foot pit, snag a grappling hook on a building ledge, hastily tie a bowline or perform a similar task requiring agility or finesse (see ***[[5. Dungeon Adventuring|Dungeon Adventuring]]***).

## Constitution (CON)
Health, stamina, and endurance.

- **Hit points:** Applies when rolling a character’s hit points (i.e., at 1st level and every time a level is gained thereafter). A character always gains at least 1 hit point per Hit Die, regardless of the CON modifier.
- **Skill:** This adjusts the target number when stamina or endurance would apply to a skill check.
- **Test of Constitution:** The target number to sprint more than 40 yards, toil in bitter cold or heat, endure a forced march up to 12 hours or perform a similar task requiring endurance or fitness (see ***[[5. Dungeon Adventuring|Dungeon Adventuring]]***).
- **Trauma Survival:** The target number on a d12 to survive a traumatic shock to the system, such as being turned to stone, polymorphed or resurrected.

## Charisma (CHA)

Leadership potential, personal magnetism, and hiding one's true intentions.

- **Reaction/Loyalty:** Applies hired henchmen and when interacting with monsters.
- **Max # Henchmen:** Determines the number of retainers a character may have at any time.
- **Skill:** This adjusts the target number when affability or deceptiveness would apply to a skill check.
- **Test of Charisma:** The target number to bribe a guard, seduce someone, lie convincingly or perform a similar task requiring leadership, deception or friendliness (see ***[[5. Dungeon Adventuring|Dungeon Adventuring]]***).
